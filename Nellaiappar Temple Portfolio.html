Temple Profile

Nellaiappar Temple is a historic Shaivite temple located in Tirunelveli, Tamil Nadu. Renowned for its ancient architecture, sacred sanctum, and vibrant festivals, the temple welcomes devotees from across the state. The compound houses the main shrine of Lord Shiva (Nellaiappar) and Goddess Parvati (Kanthimathi), with richly carved pillars, a large temple tank, and cultural heritage features.
